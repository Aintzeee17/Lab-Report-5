Our Vision

UMT aspires to be a leading university recognized for excellence in marine and fisheries research, innovative education, and community impact. Our vision emphasizes sustainable development, technological advancement, and nurturing highly skilled graduates who can contribute to national and global development.

Our Mission

The mission of UMT is to provide high-quality education, foster innovative research, and promote sustainable practices across marine, technological, and business disciplines. We aim to produce competent, ethical, and socially responsible graduates, strengthen collaborations with industry and communities, and continuously enhance our academic and research capabilities to address the challenges of the modern world.
